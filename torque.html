Torque
Definition: Torque is a rotational force around a pivot point.

τ = tau [Nm] or [Kg(m^2/s^2)]

τ = tangential Force * distance from pivot point




In this situation, the see-saw is not in motion, therefore the total torque is 0.
The torque formula will be used to solve for the distance of person B.

τ = torque of person B - torque of person A

Person A is being subtracted because the direction of their rotation is clockwise, therefore negative.
