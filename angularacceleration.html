Angular Acceleration
Angular acceleration:

α = Δω/Δt [rad/s]


Tangential acceleration:

at = vt/Δt

at = rω/Δt

at = ω/Δt * r

at = α * r

Formulas

Linear motion:

P(t) = 1/2 a (t)^2 + vo(t) + po

Vt = a(t) + vo

(Vf)^2 = (Vo)^2 + 2aΔd


Angular motion:

θ(t) = 1/2 α(t)^2 + ωo(t) + θo

ωt = α(t) + ωo

(ωf)^2 = (ωo)^2 + 2αΔθ
