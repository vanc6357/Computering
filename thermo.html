Thermodynamics
Golden rule:
Energy can not be created or destroyed. It can only be transformed.

In thermodynamics, energy is represented as work.
Formula for conservation of energy:

General formula for work:
Work = Force * distance

Thermodynamics are made up of types of work.

1. Gravitational potential energy (Ep)
W = F*d
Ep = Fg*d
Ep = m*g*h

 2. Friction energy (Wf)
W = F*d
Wf = Ff*d
W = 1/2 Miew(Fn)*d

 3. Kinetic energy(Ek) Anything in movement has kinetic energy
Ek = 1/2(m)V^2

 4. Spring energy
Es = 1/2(K)x^2 ---> K is the spring constant and x is the compression change
