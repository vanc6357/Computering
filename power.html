Power
Power is a change in energy as a function of time.

P = watts (W)
E = joules (J)
t = seconds (s)

P = E/t
P(t) = E [W*s = J]

Ex:
1000 W(s) = 1 Kw(s)
3600 000 W(s) = 1 Kw(h)
