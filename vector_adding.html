Adding vectors

1. Make a chart of the x and y components of each vector
2. Add them up, taking signs into consideration
3. Use the Pythagoras theorem with the total x and y values to calculate the magnitude of the new vector.
4. Use inverse tan to calculate the direction, in degrees, of the vector.
