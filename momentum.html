Momentum Rho = p symbol for momentum

Pic of conservation of momentum

Relation to force:
Force is the rate of change of momentum as a function of time.
How the formula F= p /delta t was acquired:

F = m*a

F = m*(v/t)

P = m * v

F = p /delta t

The slope of a p(t) graph is equal to the average Force.

Inelastic collisions:

In an inelastic collision, some kinetic energy is lost to the environment when both objects collide.
Elastic collisions:
In an elastic collision, the kinetic energy is completely distributed amongst the two objects colliding.

Example:

A 1000 kg truck collides with a 200 kg car at a velocity of 18 m/s. What is the final velocity of both objects? (Since this is an elastic collision, the sum of all final velocities should be equal to the initial velocity)
