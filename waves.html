Waves
A wave is a particle in oscillatory motion.
Sound and light both emit waves.

Sound waves

Frequency:

Number of waves in one second. [Hz]
High frequency waves are thinner, low frequency waves are wider. Frequency is related to the pitch of a sound.

Period T:
Time for one wave cycle [s]

Amplitude:
Intensity (or loudness) of the sound wave.
